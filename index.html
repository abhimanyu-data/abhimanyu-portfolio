About Me

I'm Abhimanyu Singh, a seasoned Product Leader with over 15 years of experience building and scaling AI-powered products, enterprise SaaS platforms, and data-driven solutions. I specialize in bridging the gap between strategy and execution—translating complex business needs into impactful, user-centric products.

Throughout my career, I’ve led cross-functional Agile teams and spearheaded the development of AI-driven applications including personalization engines, intelligent analytics platforms, and automated customer engagement tools. My background blends deep technical knowledge with strategic vision, enabling me to drive full product lifecycles—from ideation and roadmapping to launch and continuous improvement.

I’ve had the opportunity to guide Agile transformations, coach high-performing teams, and deliver measurable outcomes for global organizations like Dentsu, Ford Motor Company, ValueLabs, and Cognizant. Whether scaling development teams or embedding machine learning into core product features, I focus on creating products that solve real-world problems and deliver value at scale.

I'm certified as a Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), Scrum Master (CSM), and ITIL Expert, and I bring a data-informed mindset to product discovery, user research, and go-to-market execution.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about travel, music, and continuously exploring how technology can shape the future of human experiences.
